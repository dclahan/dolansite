About Dolan Clahan

I am a passionate, hard worker from Mill Valley, California. I am often working on leetcode problems, making websites, training ML models, and working on my various projects. I like to stay active and outside through hiking, surfing, running, kayaking and skateboarding. I love watching movies, especially with my friends, and am very active on LetterBoxd.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2022 with a BS in Mathematics and Computer Science with a concentration on theory and algorithms. I took many great classes... I work mostly in python, html/js, c++, etc... I am an independent worker who is very capable of figuring out problems by myself, but I love when I get opportunities to share my findings and share about my projects and to communicate with likeminded interested parties.

I am currently working as a tutor at LifeWorks Marin, where I provide aid to middle and high school students struggling in a multitude of subjects, mostly math: geometry, algebra, precalculus and calculus. It is a good opportunity to spread knowledge and help out students who don't have the material fully locked in yet, we've all been there!
